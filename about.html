About

How dreams come true…

Frontend Force (a venture of Polish company Agile Ways sp. z o. o, and German system house PLAN Solutions GmbH & Co. KG) is the proud organizer of Front Trends 2010, hosted by Paweł Czerski and Damian Wielgosik. Featuring talks by 18 of the leading industry names, the goal is to inspire creativity and explore the evolving trends of the front-end landscape.

The history

One day good fellows and great enthusiasts of the web – Paweł Czerski and Damian Wielgosik – discussed the idea of organizing the best web conference Central and Eastern Europe had ever seen. The topics: JavaScript, CSS, and Usability.

From the moment they began discussions, they were inspired and their breathing became faster – it seemed only a few steps to make their dreams come true! Then they became painfully aware of the reality that they had no sponsors, no speakers, no location. Like Bohemian Rhapsody.

They knew that had to succeed despite it all. Never before had there been an opportunity for great developers in Central and Eastern Europe to meet with other great minds from all over the world. They owed it to themselves and to the people shaping the front-end industry.

After 6 months of desperation, they met Frontend Force—a company with the same enthusiasm as Paweł and Damian for front-end web development. Together, the project slowly transformed from dream into reality. Excitement only grew as the speaker list turned into a fully matured cast of amazing international developers.

Now, the three friends are proud to present the first Front Trends 2010 Conference, an event that will bring people together and facilitate the sharing of new ideas.
