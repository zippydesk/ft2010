Take a cab

There are multiple taxi companies in Warsaw with a whole range of prices. We want you to spend as little money as possible for transport, and hence presenting a mobile app our friends are working on. Złap Taxi is a taxi finder for most of the locations in Poland. Simply use your GPS-equipped phone or select a city from the list to see the prices and phone numbers.

Currently an English/Polish version for iPhone and a Polish one for Android are available, but by the time you come for the conference, you’ll be able to download j2me versions as well—all 3 in English.

Be sure to have it installed during the conference—authors are planning a little contest with a great prize :)
